How To Build A Cleaning Plan

Assess Your Battleground

The size of your home: A studio apartment requires a different approach than a sprawling family house.
Number of occupants: More people generally means more mess!
Lifestyle habits: Do you cook every night, or mostly order takeout? Do you have pets that shed? Understanding your daily routines will help prioritize cleaning tasks.

How To Build A Cleaning Plan

Divide and Conquer

Daily Tasks: Quick wins like wiping down counters, making the bed, and putting away dishes can make a big difference in overall tidiness.
Weekly Deep Cleaning: Focus on areas that require more effort, like mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms, and dusting furniture.
Monthly Maintenance: Schedule tasks like vacuuming upholstery, cleaning appliances, and decluttering specific areas.
